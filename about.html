About BYD

Founded in November 1994, BYD is a high-tech company devoted to innovation for a better life. For nearly three decades, BYD has been a global leader in electronics, automotive, renewable energy, and rail transit industries.

BYD’s Journey and Vision

With a focus on energy acquisition, storage, and application, BYD delivers comprehensive zero-emission energy solutions. As a publicly listed company on the Hong Kong and Shenzhen Stock Exchanges, BYD recorded revenue exceeding RMB 602 billion (USD 83 billion) in 2023.

Renewable Energy

Advanced battery systems, energy storage solutions, and zero-emission electric vehicles.

Smart Technology

Integration of intelligent hardware and software for the vehicles of tomorrow.

Global Growth

Over 3 million new energy vehicles sold worldwide in 2023.

BYD’s Core Technologies

Blade Battery

The safest battery in the industry — passing nail penetration tests with a compression strength of 445 kN. Fast charging from 10% to 80% in just 33 minutes, with a range of up to 600 km and beyond.

e-Platform 3.0

A pure electric platform with integrated safety systems, futuristic aerodynamics, and an 8-in-1 powertrain. Delivers 10% higher efficiency than competitors.

DM-i & DM-p Hybrid Systems

Dual-mode hybrid systems deliver lightning-fast acceleration (0–100 km/h in 4.5s) with ultra-low fuel consumption — smooth and silent like a full EV.

Yisifang Platform (e⁴)

A four-motor independent drive system offering superior traction, precision control, and rapid adaptability across all terrains.

Achievements & Environmental Impact

On July 4, 2024, BYD became the first new energy vehicle manufacturer in the world to produce its 8 millionth NEV. By November 2023, BYD had reduced more than 44 billion kg of CO₂ emissions — equivalent to planting 740 million trees.
